💧 WATERSURE — CAP YOUR WATER BILL IF YOU'RE ON BENEFITS

WaterSure caps your water bill at the average for your area — no matter how much you use. You qualify if you're on certain benefits AND have a medical condition needing lots of water, or have 3+ children at home. Apply directly to your water supplier — it's free.
