Salary Advance — Ask Ya Employer

Ring or email ya employer and ask for a salary advance against ya next pay. Most companies can do same day or next day. No interest. No fees. Ya just getting ya own money early.

Ring ya local council and ask for the Household Support Fund or Local Welfare Provision. It is a grant. Ya never pay it back. For genuine emergencies — food, fuel, essential bills.

Ring ya bank and say ya in financial hardship. Most UK banks have hardship teams who can give fee-free overdraft buffers, payment holidays and emergency support. They don't advertise it.

WHAT DOES IT ALL MEAN? 💳

PAYDAY LOAN

Borrow £100. Pay back £200+.

Interest rates of 1,000-1,500% APR. Designed to trap ya. Every rollover makes it worse. If ya behind on one — the debt doubles fast.

⚠️ NEVER. Under any circumstances. Ring Citizens Advice instead.

OVERDRAFT

Bank lets ya go below zero.

Usually around 39% interest on arranged overdrafts. Emergency use only. Ring ya bank and ask for a fee-free hardship buffer first.

Ask ya bank: "Do ya have a fee-free overdraft buffer?"

⚠️ Emergency only. Not for regular spending.

CREDIT CARD

Buy now. Pay end of month. Free if ya clear it.

Used right — pay the FULL balance every month — costs nothing and builds ya credit score. Used wrong — minimum payments only — doubles in 3 years.
