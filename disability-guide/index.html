PIP —
PERSONAL INDEPENDENCE PAYMENT

up to £172.75 a week. tax free. ya don't need to be in a wheelchair. mental health counts.

💜 THE MOST IMPORTANT THING TO KNOW

When ya fill in ya PIP form — describe how ya condition affects ya on ya WORST day. Not ya average day. Not a good day. Ya WORST day.

Most people undersell themselves and get turned down. The question isn't "can ya do this" — it's "can ya do this safely, repeatedly, in a reasonable time, and without pain." If the answer to any of those is no — ya can't do it.

"Can ya cook a meal?"

Don't say: "Yes sometimes." Say: "On my worst days I cannot stand long enough to cook. I cannot concentrate enough to follow a recipe safely. I have dropped hot pans because of tremors."

Get help filling in the form. Do not do it alone.

Step 3 — Get evidence from ya GP

Ask ya GP for a letter supporting ya claim. Ask for it in writing. The more evidence the better. The DWP must consider it all.

Ask ya GP: "Can ya write a letter supporting my PIP claim describing how my condition affects my daily life on my worst days?"

🏠 WHO DOESN'T HAVE TO PAY

Millions of people pay council tax they legally don't owe. Nobody tells ya. Ya just keep paying.

If anyone in the household qualifies as a "disregarded person" — they don't count when calculating the bill. Get enough disregarded people and ya pay nothing at all.

🧠

Severe Mental Impairment — 100% Exempt

If ya have a severe mental impairment — including dementia, severe learning disabilities, or a condition that permanently affects intelligence and social functioning — ya may be completely exempt from council tax.

If ya live with someone who has SMI and ya the only other adult — ya also get a 25% discount automatically.

Ask ya council: "Does my condition qualify me for a severe mental impairment council tax exemption?"
Check exemptions at gov.uk → POTENTIALLY 100% FREE

Living Alone — 25% Off Automatically

If ya the only adult in ya home ya get 25% off ya council tax bill automatically. If ya not getting it — ring ya council today. Many people don't realise they qualify.

Ring ya council and say: "I live alone. Am I getting my single person discount on my council tax?"
25% OFF — AUTOMATIC
