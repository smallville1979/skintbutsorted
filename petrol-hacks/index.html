⚡ SOCIAL TARIFFS — THE FUEL DISCOUNT NOBODY TELLS YA

On benefits? Your energy supplier has a cheaper rate. They don't advertise it. You have to ask. Here's what to say.

📞 Ring your supplier and say exactly this:

"Hi, I'm on Universal Credit / PIP / ESA. Do you have a social tariff or lower rate for customers on benefits?"

💡 What Social Tariffs Actually Are (2026)

Reduced energy rates for qualifying benefit claimants. Typically saves £150–400/year. British Gas, E.ON, OVO, EDF, Octopus and Scottish Power all offer versions. Ask for "priority services" or "affordability scheme" too — they're not always labelled "social tariff."

🌡️ Warm Home Discount 2026 — £150 Off Your Bill

Pension Credit (Guarantee Credit) = automatic payment. UC, Income Support or income-related ESA = apply before the annual deadline via your supplier's website. £150 off your electricity bill. Free money — takes 5 minutes.

🏠 ECO4 — Free Insulation, New Boiler, Heat Pump

On benefits? ECO4 pays for insulation, a replacement boiler or even a heat pump — completely free. Saves hundreds per year permanently. Check eligibility at gov.uk/ecohelpinghand or ask your energy supplier: "Am I eligible for ECO4 funding?"
