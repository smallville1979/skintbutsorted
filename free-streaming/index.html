Worth Knowing

VPN — do you need one?

A VPN lets you appear to be in another country — useful for accessing region-locked content. For basic free streaming on the apps above you don't need one. If you want to access US Netflix or other region-locked content, a free VPN tier (ProtonVPN free) works for occasional use.

Free VPNs — be careful

Many free VPNs sell your browsing data. ProtonVPN's free tier is genuinely trustworthy. Avoid random free VPNs from the app store — they make money from your data, not from you.
