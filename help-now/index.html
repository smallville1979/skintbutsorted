UC ADVANCE PAYMENT — GET PAID NOW

On Universal Credit and waiting for your first payment? You can get an advance of your full first payment immediately. Log into your UC journal and request an advance, or tell your work coach at your first appointment.

Request UC advance →

Paid back over 24 months. Zero interest. You are entitled to this.

HOUSEHOLD SUPPORT FUND — FREE COUNCIL MONEY MOST PEOPLE NEVER CLAIM

Every local council in England has a Household Support Fund — cash grants for food, energy, essentials. No loan. No repayment. Just apply. Most councils run it quarterly and the money runs out — so go now, not later. Google "[your council] Household Support Fund" or call your council directly.

Also ask about emergency welfare assistance — separate pot, same idea, different name in some councils.
