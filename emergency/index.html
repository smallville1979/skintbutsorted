CREDIT 101 💳

nobody taught us this. here it is. simple. no jargon.

ZILCH

Buy now. Pay in 6 weeks. 0% interest.

Split any purchase into 4 payments over 6 weeks. No credit card needed. No interest ever. Good for emergencies when ya skint but ya know money is coming.

Get Zilch →

⚠️ Only use if ya KNOW ya can pay it back. Don't stack up buy-now-pay-later debt.

OVERDRAFT

Bank lets ya go below zero.

Ya spend more than ya have. Bank covers it — but usually charges interest (around 39%). If ya going overdrawn call ya bank first and ask about a fee-free buffer. Most offer one.

"Do you have a fee-free overdraft buffer for hardship?"

⚠️ Overdraft interest is brutal. Emergency only.

CREDIT CARD

Buy now. Pay end of month. 0% if ya clear it.

Used right — pay the FULL balance every month — a credit card costs ya nothing and builds ya credit score. Used wrong — minimum payments only — the debt doubles in 3 years.

Find best 0% cards →

⚠️ Golden rule: only spend what ya already have in ya bank.

PAYDAY LOAN

AVOID. 1,500% APR. Debt trap.

Payday lenders charge up to 1,500% APR. £100 borrowed can become £200+ in weeks. They are designed to trap ya. Use Citizens Advice instead. Always.

Call Citizens Advice free: 0800 144 8848 →

⚠️ Never. Under any circumstances.

Cheap ethical loans for low incomes.

Credit unions offer fair loans at low interest specifically for people on low incomes. Much better than payday lenders. Find ya local one at findyourcreditunion.co.uk
