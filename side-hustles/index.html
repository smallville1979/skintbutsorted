🚨 Avoid These

Side Hustle Scams.
Spot Them Fast.

The internet is full of fake gurus selling courses about the side hustle instead of just doing the side hustle. Here's what to avoid.

🚨 Red Flags — Walk Away

✗
Pay to join / "starter kit" required — Any side hustle that asks you to pay money to start making money is a scam or MLM. Legitimate side hustles cost nothing to join.
✗
"£500/day from your phone" — If it sounds too easy, it's either a lie or it requires unpaid work before you see a penny. Real hustles take real effort.
✗
MLM / Network Marketing — Herbalife, Amway, Forever Living, Juice Plus. You will spend more than you earn. The product is recruitment, not the thing they're selling. Walk away every time.
✗
Dropshipping courses — Dropshipping itself can work, but anyone selling you a £997 course to learn it is making money from the course, not the dropshipping. YouTube is free.
✗
Crypto trading courses — "Learn to trade crypto" courses from influencers are almost universally a way to get your money. Crypto is high risk. Learning from a paid course won't change that.
Rule of thumb: If someone is getting rich by teaching you how to get rich — the product is you, not the method. The real side hustles are simple: deliver food, sell things, do surveys, walk dogs, make TikToks. No course needed.
